Redesigning Lumosity: Addressing Inequalities

Lumosity is a brain-training platform. This project aimed to redesign it to be more inclusive and equitable, focusing on racial and income disparities.

Problem & Solution

Problem

  • Lack of cultural relevance in games.
  • Membership costs limiting access for low-income users.

Solution

  • Free Simplified Reports
  • Culturally Relevant Games
  • Personalized Social Play
  • Mindfulness Game Category
  • Game Recommendation System

Design Process & Outcome

An iterative, user-centered design process led to a final prototype that prioritizes inclusivity and accessibility.

View Final Prototype

Reflection

This project demonstrates the power of user-centered design to promote more equitable digital experiences. The iterative process, incorporating user feedback and learning science, led to a Lumosity redesign that is more accessible and inclusive. We learned that early user feedback can sometimes be too general to drive specific design changes, and that it’s essential to balance user input with design expertise and creativity. Through consistent refinement and a commitment to applying UI principles, we were able to significantly improve the prototype's consistency, usability, and overall professionalism.
